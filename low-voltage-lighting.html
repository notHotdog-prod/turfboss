Common Questions

Lighting FAQ

Is low voltage lighting safe?+

Absolutely. Low voltage landscape lighting operates at 12–24 volts, which is completely safe to touch and poses no electric shock risk. It's ideal for homes with children and pets.

Can I install landscape lighting myself?+

While low voltage systems are safer than line voltage, professional installation ensures proper wire routing, fixture placement for optimal aesthetics, transformer sizing, and weatherproofing. Our installations come with a full warranty.

How much does landscape lighting cost?+

Lighting systems vary widely based on the number of fixtures, wire runs, and complexity. A typical residential system ranges from $1,500 to $5,000+. We provide free design consultations and detailed estimates.

How long do LED lamps last?+

Quality LED landscape lighting lamps last 50,000+ hours — that's over 15 years of average use. This means very low ongoing maintenance and replacement costs.

Can I expand the system later?+

Yes! Low voltage systems are designed to be expandable. We size your transformer with room for future fixtures, making it easy to add lighting as your landscape evolves.
