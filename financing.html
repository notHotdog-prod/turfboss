Financing FAQ

What credit score do I need to qualify?+

Credit requirements vary by lender and plan. Some plans are available for credit scores as low as 580. The Hearth platform checks multiple lenders to find options that match your credit profile.

Does applying affect my credit score?+

Checking your rate with Hearth uses a soft credit inquiry, which does NOT impact your credit score. Only when you formally accept a loan offer will a hard inquiry be made.

How long does the application take?+

Most applicants complete the online application in under 5 minutes. Decisions are typically received within seconds to minutes.

Can I use financing for any size project?+

Financing is available for projects typically starting at $2,000. There's no upper limit — larger projects are easily financed over longer terms.

Is there a prepayment penalty?+

Most of our financing plans have no prepayment penalty. You can pay off your balance early without any additional fees.
