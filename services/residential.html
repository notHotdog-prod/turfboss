FAQ

Common Questions

How much does residential turf cost in NJ?+

Residential installations typically range from $8–$17 per square foot installed, depending on the grade of turf and complexity of the project. We provide free, detailed written estimates.

Will my HOA approve artificial turf?+

Many NJ HOAs accept properly installed artificial turf. We can provide documentation on our products and installation standards to support your HOA approval process.

What about drainage around my foundation?+

We design drainage as part of every installation. Proper base grading ensures water moves away from your foundation, just as it would with natural grass or hardscaping.

Can turf be installed around existing landscaping?+

Absolutely. We work around trees, shrubs, garden beds, and existing hardscape to create a seamless, integrated look.
