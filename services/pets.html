FAQ

Common Questions

Is artificial turf safe for pets?+

Yes. Our pet turf products are non-toxic, lead-free, and thoroughly tested for pet safety. The antimicrobial infill is also safe for pets and won't harm them if they happen to mouth the turf.

How do I clean up after my dog?+

Solid waste: remove with a bag and dispose normally. Liquid waste: the drainage system handles it — just rinse with a hose periodically for fresh water flush. Weekly enzymatic cleaner keeps odors at bay.

Will my dog overheat on artificial turf in summer?+

Heat can build up on any surface in direct sun. We offer cooling infill options and recommend providing shade, fresh water, and hosing down the turf during very hot days. The turf cools quickly when wet.

Can cats use artificial turf too?+

Absolutely. Cats enjoy artificial turf for outdoor enclosures (catios), garden spaces, and more. The same benefits apply — easy cleanup, no mud, durable surface.
