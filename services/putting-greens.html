Putting Greens

Practice your short game in your own backyard, year-round. Custom designs from 200 to 2,000+ sq ft.

For Golfers

Your Own Backyard Putting Green

If you play, you know the drill. Work all week, squeeze in a round on Saturday, make ten putts inside six feet the whole day. A backyard green fixes that. Practice before coffee, practice after dinner, practice in February. No tee times, no club fees, no excuses.

Putting green turf is a different animal from landscape turf — short pile (⅜"–¾"), dense backing, directional nap for a true roll, and we can build it to whatever speed you like. Guys who play Metedeconk, Manasquan River, or TPC Jasna Polana can usually tell the difference in the first few rolls.

  • True, consistent ball roll that mirrors real course conditions
  • Customizable speed rating to match your preferred course conditions
  • Multiple hole cup placements for full practice variety
  • Optional fringe, rough, and bunker areas for complete short game
  • Available with lighting for night putting sessions
  • Looks stunning year-round — a backyard showpiece

FAQ

Common Questions

How much does a backyard putting green cost?+

Putting green installations typically range from $12–$24 per square foot installed, depending on size, contour complexity, number of holes, and any add-ons. A 500 sq ft green might start around $6,000.

Can I get multiple holes?+

Absolutely. Most of our designs include 2–5 hole placements, allowing you to practice a variety of distances and angles from different starting positions.

How fast is the green? Can I adjust the speed?+

We can customize the stimp speed (how fast the ball rolls) during installation through infill type and density. Speeds from slower practice conditions to tour-speed greens are achievable.

Does the putting green require maintenance?+

Minimal — occasional brushing to keep fibers aligned, and rinsing to remove debris. No mowing, no watering, no aeration. Just golf.
