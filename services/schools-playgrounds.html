Schools & Playgrounds

Safe, durable, low-maintenance turf for NJ schools, daycares, and playgrounds that meets all safety standards.

Education & Recreation

Where Kids Play Safely

The safety of children is paramount. Our school and playground turf systems go far beyond standard artificial turf — they're engineered specifically to meet ASTM F1292 safety standards for impact attenuation, with shock-absorbing underlayment rated for falls from up to 8 feet.

For NJ school boards, daycare operators, and parks departments: eliminating natural grass from playground areas means eliminating irrigation systems, weekly mowing, seasonal reseeding, and the mud that turns play areas into disaster zones after every rainfall. One installation, years of savings.

  • ASTM F1292 certified — rated for falls from up to 8 feet
  • Non-toxic, lead-free, and thoroughly tested for child safety
  • Antimicrobial treatment inhibits bacteria and mold growth
  • Mud-free surface keeps children cleaner and facilities tidier
  • No irrigation required — eliminate water costs and system maintenance
  • ADA-compliant for inclusive play environments

FAQ

Common Questions

Does playground turf meet NJ safety regulations?+

Yes. Our playground systems are ASTM F1292 certified for impact attenuation and meet NJ Department of Community Affairs playground safety guidelines. We can provide full documentation.

Is crumb rubber infill required?+

No. We offer alternative infills including organic options and silica sand, which many schools prefer for child safety reasons. We'll recommend the best option for your specific application.

How does it handle heavy rain use?+

Our drainage systems handle 1,000+ inches per hour. After heavy rain, the surface drains within minutes, allowing children to play almost immediately after precipitation stops.

Can you install around existing playground equipment?+

Absolutely. We work around all types of existing play structures and equipment, seamlessly integrating turf into the play area design.
