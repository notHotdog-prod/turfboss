Apartments & HOAs

Eliminate landscaping contracts, save on water costs, and maintain perfect common areas — year-round, with zero effort.

Property Management

The Smart Choice for Community Properties

Most HOA boards and apartment managers we work with are paying somewhere between $8,000 and $15,000 a year just to keep the common grass alive — mowing crews, irrigation, water bills, patch jobs after every drought. Artificial turf knocks most of that off the budget, permanently.

The pitch to your residents is simple: green common areas every day of the year. No muddy patches after a nor'easter, no brown stretches in August, no roped-off lawns while the reseeding takes. Just a sharp, clean look that makes the property feel cared for — without anyone having to actually care for it.

  • Eliminate $8,000–$15,000+/year in typical HOA landscaping costs
  • Consistent, pristine appearance regardless of season or drought
  • No irrigation system required — eliminate water costs entirely
  • Increased property values and community appeal for residents
  • Bulk pricing and multi-phase installation for large communities
  • Satisfies HOA aesthetic standards with upscale appearance

FAQ

Common Questions

How does the cost compare to our current landscaping contract?+

Most HOA common areas pay back the installation cost within 2–4 years through eliminated service contracts and water costs. We can provide a detailed ROI analysis for your community.

Can you phase the installation over multiple years?+

Yes. Many HOAs prefer to phase large installations over 2–3 years to spread capital costs. We can develop a phased plan that fits your budget cycle.

How do we get HOA board approval?+

We provide complete product documentation, installation specifications, warranty information, and reference photos to help you present the proposal to your HOA board. We've supported many successful NJ HOA approvals.

Is there a minimum project size?+

We welcome all project sizes but offer the best pricing on community-scale installations of 5,000+ sq ft. Contact us to discuss your community's specific needs.
