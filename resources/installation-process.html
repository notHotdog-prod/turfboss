Our Installation Process

See exactly what happens from site prep to final walkthrough — and why professional installation makes all the difference.

8 Steps to a Perfect Lawn

Professional Installation by Turf Boss NJ

Most installations are completed in just 1–2 days. Here's exactly what happens.

1

📐 Site Measurement & Assessment

We measure your space precisely, assess soil conditions, drainage, slope, and existing irrigation. This informs every decision that follows.

2

🗑️ Existing Surface Removal

Existing grass, roots, and debris are removed using professional sod cutters. All material is hauled away, leaving a clean worksite.

3

🪨 Base Preparation

We excavate 3–4 inches and install a compacted aggregate base (decomposed granite or crushed stone) that provides drainage and a stable foundation. Compacted to 90%+ Proctor density.

4

📏 Weed Barrier Installation

Heavy-duty landscape fabric is installed to prevent weed intrusion through the base over time — an often-skipped step that separates professional from amateur installs.

5

✂️ Turf Cutting & Seaming

Turf panels are measured and cut precisely. Seams are secured with seaming tape and specialized adhesive — invisible when done correctly. This is where experience truly matters.

6

🔧 Securing & Nailing

Perimeter nails and staples anchor the turf firmly at all edges and transitions. Hardscape edges are secured with specialty trim or bender board for a clean, professional finish.

7

🪨 Infill Application

Silica sand or specialty infill is power-broomed into the turf fibers. Infill provides fiber support, weight, cushioning, and helps regulate temperature. This step is critical to long-term performance.

8

✨ Final Grooming & Walkthrough

A power broom lifts fibers to their natural upright position. We perform a full inspection and then walk the finished installation with you, ensuring every detail meets our standards and yours.
