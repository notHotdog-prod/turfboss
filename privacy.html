Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information when you work with Turf Boss NJ.

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

This website is operated by Turf Boss NJ. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us when requesting a quote, scheduling a consultation, or using our services.

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us, request a quote, or work with us on a project, we may collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, and property address
  • Project details — square footage, intended use (residential, pets, putting green, commercial, etc.), and design preferences
  • Photos or measurements you share to help us scope the job
  • Financial and payment information (processed securely through our payment and financing partners)
  • Communications history — emails, texts, and notes from consultations
  • Website usage data via cookies and analytics
  • IP address, browser type, and device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to quote requests and schedule consultations or installations
  • Send project updates, appointment confirmations, and post-installation follow-ups
  • Process payments and financing applications through partners like Hearth
  • Provide warranty service and maintenance support
  • Improve our website, marketing, and customer experience
  • Comply with legal, tax, and regulatory obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may share information with trusted partners only as needed to deliver our services, including:

  • Turf manufacturers and suppliers (for warranty registration and product fulfillment)
  • Subcontractors involved in your specific project (e.g., licensed electricians for lighting work)
  • Payment processors and financing partners
  • Hosting, CRM, and email service providers that support our day-to-day operations
  • Analytics and advertising platforms (in aggregated or anonymized form where possible)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. SMS and Text Messaging Privacy

If you opt in to receive SMS or text messages from Turf Boss NJ, the following applies in addition to the rest of this Privacy Policy:

No sharing of mobile information. We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise disclose your mobile phone number, SMS opt-in consent records, or any data related to your SMS interactions with us to any third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes. This restriction applies regardless of the third party's identity or relationship to us. Mobile information is not shared with third parties for marketing under any circumstances.

What we collect for SMS: Mobile phone number, opt-in source (contact form, quote request, or in-person), timestamp, IP address at time of opt-in, and message content/timestamps for messages sent and received.

How we use it: Solely to send appointment confirmations, appointment reminders, quote follow-ups, and customer service messages you have opted in to receive, and to maintain compliance records as required by federal and state law.

Sub-processors. SMS delivery is handled by Twilio Inc. and our configured messaging service providers, who process this data only to deliver messages on our behalf and are contractually prohibited from using it for any other purpose.

Your rights. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may request deletion of your phone number and SMS consent records by emailing info@turfbossnj.com or calling (732) 800-6095.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, understand how visitors use our site, and measure the effectiveness of our marketing. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality and your experience.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Under New Jersey privacy laws and federal regulations, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of promotional emails and text messages
  • Request a portable copy of your data
  • Object to certain data processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@turfbossnj.com or (732) 800-6095.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your information. We follow industry best practices for security. However, no system is completely secure. We encourage strong passwords and prompt reporting of any suspicious activity.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our services are for homeowners, property managers, and business operators — not children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover a child has provided information, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians may request removal by contacting us.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (financing partners, manufacturers, social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date, and your continued use of our services indicates acceptance of the changes.
