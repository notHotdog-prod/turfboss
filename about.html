About Turf Boss NJ

Family-owned, Ocean County-based, and committed to transforming New Jersey outdoor spaces one lawn at a time.

Our Story

Born and Raised in Ocean County

Turf Boss NJ was founded in 2014 by Kirk, a lifelong Ocean County resident who had a simple vision: give New Jersey homeowners and businesses a better alternative to the endless grind of lawn maintenance. After years of seeing neighbors struggle with brown patches, water bills, and weekend hours spent behind a mower, Kirk James set out to bring premium artificial turf solutions to the shore communities he called home.

What started as a one-man operation has grown into one of Ocean County's most trusted turf installation companies, with over 500 completed projects across New Jersey. From Toms River to Long Beach Island, Brick to Barnegat — Turf Boss NJ has transformed outdoor spaces of every kind.

We're not a franchise or a big-box contractor. We're a local team who takes pride in our work, stands behind every installation, and treats every client's property like it's our own.
